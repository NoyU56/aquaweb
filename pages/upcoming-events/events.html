Add-On-Activities:

  • Get nose-to-beak with one of our Humboldt penguins! Observe our penguins up close and learn all about these incredible animals!

    To ensure the welfare and safety of our penguins, no touching will be permitted.

  • Provide your guests with a unique experience as they are given the opportunity to meet many of our invertebrates who call the Aquarium their home! Our Touch Tank Experience includes hands-on interactions with ocean-critters including sea stars, sea urchins, hermit crabs, and horseshoe crabs.

  • Interact with the Aquarium’s freshwater turtles while learning all about them! Participants will have the opportunity to touch a turtle and ask their caretakers questions!
